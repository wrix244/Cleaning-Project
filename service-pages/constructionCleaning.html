Construction Cleaning Services in Melbourne

Imp Cleaning Services specialises in post-construction cleaning for residential, commercial, and industrial properties. After construction or renovation, spaces are often left covered in dust, debris, and leftover materials. Our professional construction cleaning team ensures that your property is spotless, safe, and ready for occupancy.

Our skilled cleaners handle every corner, removing dust from surfaces, scrubbing floors, cleaning windows, and disposing of debris responsibly. We follow strict safety protocols and use advanced equipment to achieve a thorough and efficient clean.

Our Construction Cleaning Services Include:

  • Dust and debris removal from all surfaces
  • Scrubbing and polishing of floors
  • Window cleaning (interior and exterior)
  • Kitchen and bathroom sanitation
  • Post-renovation rubbish removal
  • Removal of stickers, paint spots, and adhesive residues
  • Final inspection to ensure readiness for handover

Whether it’s a small renovation or a large construction site, Imp Cleaning Services is ready to clean anywhere in Melbourne. We guarantee a pristine environment so you can hand over or move into your property without worry.
